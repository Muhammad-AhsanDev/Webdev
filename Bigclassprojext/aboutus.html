About Us

Welcome to our Class Progress Tracker — a collaborative space built by the students of the Modern Web and App Development Course at SMIT Gulshan Campus. This website is designed to showcase the hard work, growth, and achievements of every student in our class.

Our goal is to create a transparent and engaging platform where students can track their learning progress, display their portfolios, and get motivated by each other’s milestones. From coding projects to creative web designs, every student’s effort is recognized and celebrated here.

This project reflects what we’re learning — how to design, develop, and deploy real-world web applications using modern technologies. It’s not just a website; it’s a step towards becoming skilled developers ready to build the future of the web.

Together, we’re learning, building, and growing — one project at a time.
